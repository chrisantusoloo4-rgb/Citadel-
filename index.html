Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about enrolling, attending, and thriving at Citadel Academy.

How do I enroll my child in the holiday tuition program? +
Simply contact us via WhatsApp on 0728 528 654 or fill out our online inquiry form. Our team will guide you through the registration process and confirm your child's placement within 24 hours.
What subjects are covered in the holiday tuition? +
We cover all major KCSE subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Geography, History, English, Literature in English, Kiswahili, CRE, Computer Studies, and French — all taught by subject specialists.
How do the online tutoring sessions work? +
Online sessions are conducted live via Zoom or Google Meet. Once enrolled, you'll receive a scheduled meeting link. Sessions are interactive, personalized, and can be recorded for review at your convenience.
Can international students join the holiday tuition in person? +
Yes! International students and diaspora families are welcome to attend in person at our Bondo Township location. We recommend reaching out early to plan accommodation and logistics well in advance.
How are payments made for the programs? +
Payments for local programs are accepted via M-Pesa. For international online tutoring, we accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and digital payment platforms. Full payment details are provided upon enrollment confirmation.
Is the mentorship program available online? +
Yes. Our mentorship sessions are available both in-person at our Bondo Township campus and online via video call. We accommodate the needs and location of each client. Note that transport and meals for in-person sessions are excluded from the KES 5,000 fee.
What is the schedule for recreational activities? +
Recreation — including swimming, football, and photography — takes place after the day's academic sessions. The schedule is structured to ensure students are refreshed and energized while still maintaining rigorous academic focus.
Do you offer KCSE-specific preparation? +
Absolutely. Our entire local holiday tuition curriculum is designed around KCSE requirements. Our tutors use past papers, marking schemes, and targeted revision strategies proven to improve student performance in national exams.
